NatCam.SaveToPhotos

public static void SaveToPhotos (Texture2D photo, PhotoSaveMode saveMode);

Description

Save a photo to the camera roll.

This is unsupported on the fallback interface. 

//Save a photo to our app's album in the camera roll
NatCam.SaveToPhotos(photo, PhotoSaveMode.SaveToAppAlbum);

See Also: PhotoSaveMode .
